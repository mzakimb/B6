About Me

My name is Joseph A. Cooper, I am a wildlife photographer and humanitarian. I was born on September 13, 1951 in Seattle. I graduated from the University of Washington with Bachelor's degrees in fine arts and art education in 1975, where I studied under professors such as Jacob Lawrence. My photographic mission is multi-faceted. By employing artistic and journalistic styles, he documents his subjects and educates the viewer.
